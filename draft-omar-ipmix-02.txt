            Internet Protocol Mixture (IPmix) Specification
                          draft-omar-ipmix-02

Abstract

   This document specifies the Internet Protocol Mixture (IPmix). a
   solution that allows IPv4-only hosts to communicate with IPv6-only
   hosts and vice versa.
