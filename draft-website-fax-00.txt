                 Extensions of httpd or webserver to allow configruing incoming FAX by ISP's
                         draft-website-fax-00.txt


Abstract

    This document describes the need for a FAX recieving services that can
    be easily supported by a ISP who can afford a fax machine and those who
    own domains and websites using ISPs should be able to recieve FAX.
