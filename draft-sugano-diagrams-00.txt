Rosenberg for the IMPP Working Group (draft-ietf-impp-model-01.txt).

Fig 2: Basic Model for Instant Message Service
When sending an IM, the sender must be an entity because an INSTANT MESSAGE
SERVICE might need to authenticate the sender. At the implementation level,
the sender will usually coincide with a PRESENTITY. So, here we propose to
introduce a notion of SENDER in the model.
