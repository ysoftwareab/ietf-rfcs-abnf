1 Time Zone Abbreviations
1.1 Commonly used abbreviations

   Some commonly abbreviations used for Brazilian timezones are: EST,
   EDT, BRT, BRST, BRDT and others.
   There is even an ambiguity, in some implementations BRST is -3:00,
   in others BRST is -2:00.

1.2 Recommended Abbreviations

   This document recommends the usage of the following abbreviations:

   Standard-Time  Daylight-Saving-Time  Example-Cities
   BREST -2:00    BREDT -1:00           Some eastern islands
   BRST  -3:00    BRDT  -2:00           Brasilia, Sao Paulo, Rio de
                                        Janeiro, Belo Horizonte
   BRWST -4:00    BRWDT -3:00           Manaus, Cuiaba
   BRAST -5:00    BRADT -4:00           Rio Branco

   Abbreaviations meaning are:
     BRE..  ->  BRazil East
     BR..   ->  BRazil (standard Brazilian time, Brasilia city)
     BRW..  ->  BRazil West
     BRA..  ->  BRazil Acre state
     ...ST  ->  Standard Time
     ...DT  ->  Daylight-saving Time
