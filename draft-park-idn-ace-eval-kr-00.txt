Abstract

    This document explains about result of applying various ACEs to existing Hangeul Domain Names
    and considers which ACE is appropriate to Hangeul Domain Names.


1. The test data composition

    The data for this ACE test consist of two lists. 
      - 1000 Hageul domain names
      - 1000 real Korean company names.
      - 1000 web site names
      (Company Names and Web site names are very strong Hangeul Domain Name Candidates)
     
    Hangeul Domain Names consist of at least 2 characters
    Hangeul Domain Names Names may include letters, digits and hyphens, but at least 1 Hangeul character.

4. The summary of result 
    
    4-1 ACEed Hangeul Domain Name Length
    
     - The Maximum length of Hangeul Domain Name before the conversion

      UTF-6   RACE   LACE   BRACE   DUDE   AltDUDE   AMC-ACE-M   AMC-ACE-O   AMC-ACE-R
       14            17       17         17          14        14            19                     18                    15
    
      - Good : AME-ACE-M, AMC-ACE-O
      - Bad   : UTF6, DUDE(AltDUDE)
      
    4-2 Conversion Speed
      - Good : AltDUDE, DUDE
      - Bad   : AMC-ACE-O, AMC-ACE-M
      
       
  
5. Conclusion

   For Hangeul Domain Name, AME-ACE-M seems to be the best choice, if we think the length is more important 
   than the conversion speed.
   DUDE may be a good choce if the ACEed length of Hangeul Domain Name can be shortened.
